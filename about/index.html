About

My name is Cody Zanga and I’m currently a senior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I’ll be graduating this fall with a major in sports media and communication with a minor in statistics.

I’m originally from Renton, Wash. and grew up a fan of all the Seattle sports teams. I’ve been fascinated with looking at statistics in sports ever since I was a kid. I plan on analyzing data on all sorts of different sports and teams, but especially my Seattle Mariners.
